How to play

When you click start, a 60 second timer will begin, and a multiplication question will pop up. Your goal is to complete as many questions correctly as possible before time runs out! You will get your results at the end. Points = Correct Questions - Incorrect Questions.



Some tips to be faster at multiplication

Have you noticed you are lacking or quite slow at the times tables? See the info below to see how to improve

Multiplication, and the times tables aren't just about memorising everything in the table. Contrary to common belief, ypu do not need to memorise much. A much better way to learn is by using 'tricks' to work out the times tables quickly...

Common Tricks

  • 9x Tables: This is a fairly complex trick, but is good once you understand.
    9x7 = 63. 6+3 = 9. 7 - 1 = 6. As you can see, to find a result, do y (where y is 9 times y) y minus 1, then find the difference between y-1 and 9 (e.g. if y = 7, 7-1 = 6. Therefore, 9 - 6 = 3. ) Then put the numbers together. (continuing with the previous example, 6 was the first number, 3 was the last. Can you see it? 9X7 = 63)
